- **Regulatory-first approach** as a trust differentiator

### 1.2 IIFL Crisis: Strategic Lessons

**Root Causes of IIFL's RBI Ban (March 2024):**

| Violation Category | What Happened | Lesson for Kotak |
|-------------------|---------------|------------------|
| **Gold Valuation** | Overvaluation to increase loan amounts | Implement dual-verification (AI + manual) |
| **LTV Breaches** | Exceeded 75% cap systematically | Real-time LTV monitoring dashboards |
| **Cash Transactions** | Violated Rs 20,000 limit | Digital-only disbursement above threshold |
| **Auction Process** | Irregular gold auctions | Standardized auction SOPs with audit trails |
| **Fintech Partnerships** | Inadequate oversight of partners | FLDG caps, regular partner audits |
| **Documentation** | Incomplete customer records | Mandatory e-KYC, digital document storage |

**Impact on IIFL:**
- Stock crashed 37% in 3 days
- Gold loan portfolio declined 53%
- 1M+ customers displaced
- Rs 2,500+ crore market cap erosion

**Strategic Implication:** Position Kotak as the "compliance-first" gold lender. Trust = Competitive Advantage.
