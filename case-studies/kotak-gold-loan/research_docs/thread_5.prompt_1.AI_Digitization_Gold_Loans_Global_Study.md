# AI & Digitization in Gold Loan Industry: Global Study

**Research Date:** December 22, 2025
**Objective:** Comprehensive analysis of AI and digitization use cases in the gold loan industry globally, focusing on India (AI only) and countries with similar demographic/economic conditions (digitization focus)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This report analyzes AI applications and digital transformation in the gold loan industry across India, Southeast Asia, Middle East, Africa, and Latin America. Key findings include:

- **AI in Gold Loans (India)**: Mature ecosystem with AI-powered purity verification (XRF + computer vision), automated credit assessment, chatbots, and fraud detection
- **Digitization Leaders**: Indonesia (Pegadaian), Philippines (PawnHero), Thailand (Easy Money) leading in Southeast Asia
- **Emerging Markets**: Middle East (UAE gold loans), Africa (mobile money integration), Latin America (broader fintech lending)
- **Global Pawn Market**: USD 34.95 billion (2024), growing at 3.6% CAGR, with 21% transactions now digital
- **Key Technologies**: XRF analyzers with AI, computer vision, blockchain tokenization, automated pawn machines (APMs)
