## 2. Product Launch: "Switch & Save" Balance Transfer by Q1 2025

### Product Overview:

**Product Name**: Switch & Save Gold Loan Balance Transfer

**Target Segment**: Customers with active gold loans at NBFCs (Muthoot, Manappuram, IIFL, others)

**Value Proposition**:
- **Up to 40% lower interest rates** vs NBFC rates
- **Zero balance transfer fees** (limited time offer)
- **Same-day approval**, 24-48 hour disbursement
- **No foreclosure charges** from Kotak if customer exits early
- **Bank-grade security** and compliance

**Product Features**:
- Loan Amount: Rs 25,000 - Rs 1 Crore
- Interest Rate: 9.5% - 10.5% (vs NBFC 16-21%)
- LTV: Up to 75% (as per RBI norms)
- Tenure: 12 months (renewable)
- Processing Fee: 1% + GST (waived for first 5,000 customers)

**Eligibility**:
- Existing gold loan from competitor (active or recently closed)
- Minimum 6 months repayment history
- No current defaults (0 DPD at time of application)
- Gold purity: 18K-24K (BIS certified testing)

**Launch Timeline**:
- **Approval Today**: December 2025
- **Product Development**: January 2025 (systems, workflows, documentation)
- **Pilot Launch**: February 2025 (5 branches in Mumbai/Bangalore)
- **Full Launch**: March 2025 (all 250 branches)

**Success Criteria**:
- 5,000 customers by June 2025
- Average ticket size Rs 3 lakh
- CAC <Rs 2,500
- NPA <0.5% at 6 months

**Approval Sought**: Green-light "Switch & Save" product for Q1 2025 launch with authority to waive processing fees for first 5,000 customers (revenue impact: Rs 1.5 Cr).

**Product Owner**: GM, Gold Loan Products (reporting to Head of Retail Banking)

## 3. AI Development: Initiate CV Appraisal + Predictive Model Build

### AI Initiative Overview:

**Objective**: Deploy AI-powered gold appraisal and customer risk prediction to achieve 70% automation by Month 9.

### Component 1: Computer Vision (CV) Gold Appraisal System

**Purpose**: Automate gold purity and weight verification using image recognition

**Technology Approach**:
- Partner with proven fintech vendor (e.g., Perfios, KredX, or custom build)
- Train AI model on 50,000+ gold ornament images
- Integrate with BIS equipment for hallmark verification
- Real-time purity estimation (±2% accuracy)

**Implementation Timeline**:
- Vendor selection: January 2025
- Model development: February-April 2025
- Pilot testing: May 2025 (5 branches, 500 loans)
- Full rollout: June-August 2025

**Expected Outcomes**:
- 70% of appraisals automated (simple hallmarked gold)
- 30% manual review (antique, non-hallmarked, complex items)
- Appraisal time reduced from 30 mins to 5 mins
- Consistency improved (remove human subjectivity)

**Investment**: Rs 8 Cr (Year 1)

### Component 2: Predictive Analytics for Switch Propensity & Default Risk

**Purpose**: Identify high-propensity switchers from existing customer base and predict default risk

**Models to Build**:

1. **Switch Propensity Model**:
   - Input: Customer demographics, transaction patterns, NBFC loan indicators, financial stress signals
   - Output: Probability score (0-100) for likelihood to switch from NBFC to bank gold loan
   - Use Case: Target marketing, personalized offers, sales prioritization

2. **Default Prediction Model**:
   - Input: Loan characteristics, customer financials, gold price volatility, macroeconomic factors
   - Output: Probability of default at 30, 60, 90 days
   - Use Case: Underwriting decision support, early warning system, portfolio monitoring

**Data Sources**:
- Kotak customer transaction data (with consent)
- External bureau data (CIBIL, Experian)
- Gold price feeds (MCX, IBJA)
- Demographic and geographic data

**Implementation Timeline**:
- Data pipeline setup: January-February 2025
- Model development: March-May 2025
- Backtesting and validation: June 2025
- Production deployment: July 2025

**Expected Outcomes**:
- 3x improvement in lead conversion (18% vs 6% baseline)
- 40% reduction in NPA (0.3% vs 0.5% industry average)
- Rs 1,000 reduction in CAC (targeted marketing)

**Investment**: Rs 10 Cr (Year 1)

### Governance:

**Technology Partner**: CTO office to lead vendor evaluation and selection

**AI Ethics & Risk**: Chief Risk Officer to approve models before production deployment

**Performance Monitoring**: Monthly review of model accuracy, bias, and business impact

**Approval Sought**: Initiate AI development program with Rs 18 Cr Year 1 technology budget and authority to engage external vendors.

## 5. Doorstep Pilot: Authorize 5-City Pilot Program

### Doorstep Service Overview:

**Objective**: Offer doorstep gold collection for high-value loans (Rs 5 lakh+) to match NBFC convenience without compromising security.

**Service Description**:
- Customer applies online or via app
- Kotak representative visits customer location with BIS equipment
- Gold appraisal conducted on-site
- Gold collected and transported to bank vault
- Loan disbursed within 24 hours

### Pilot Program Details:

**Pilot Cities** (5):
1. Mumbai
2. Delhi
3. Bangalore
4. Hyderabad
5. Chennai

**Rationale**: High gold loan demand, mature markets, Kotak branch presence, logistics infrastructure

**Pilot Scale**:
- **Target**: 500 doorstep loans in 6 months (Month 6-9 of Year 1)
- **Ticket Size**: Minimum Rs 5 lakh (average Rs 7 lakh)
- **Total AUM**: Rs 35 Cr from pilot

**Eligibility**:
- Existing Kotak customer (account >6 months)
- Loan amount: Rs 5 lakh - Rs 25 lakh
- Serviceable area: Within 10 km of designated Kotak branch
- Daytime collection only (9 AM - 6 PM)

### Operational Model:

**Team Structure**:
- 2-person team per collection (gold appraiser + security escort)
- 5 teams per city (10 teams total, 50 staff)
- Dedicated vehicles with GPS tracking and live video streaming

**Security Protocol**:
- Insured transportation (coverage: 100% of gold value + 25% buffer)
- Real-time GPS tracking and panic button
- Direct transport to bank vault (no intermediate stops)
- Vault deposit within 4 hours of collection
- Customer SMS/email confirmation upon vault storage

**Cost Structure**:

| Cost Element | Per Loan Cost |
|--------------|---------------|
| Staff Cost (appraiser + escort) | Rs 1,200 |
| Vehicle & Fuel | Rs 800 |
| Insurance | Rs 500 |
| Equipment (BIS portable) | Rs 200 |
| **Total Cost per Loan** | **Rs 2,700** |

**Pilot Investment**: Rs 3 Cr (Year 1)
- Staff hiring and training: Rs 1.5 Cr
- Vehicles and equipment: Rs 0.8 Cr
- Insurance: Rs 0.5 Cr
- Contingency: Rs 0.2 Cr

### Pilot Success Criteria:

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Loans Disbursed | 500 |
| Customer Satisfaction (NPS) | >60 |
| Incident-Free Operations | 100% (zero gold loss/theft) |
| Turnaround Time (application to disbursal) | <24 hours (80% of cases) |
| Cost per Loan | <Rs 3,000 |

### Scale Decision:

**If pilot succeeds** (meets all success criteria):
- Scale to 20 cities in Year 2
- Expand eligibility to Rs 3 lakh minimum ticket
- Allocate Rs 15 Cr for Year 2 expansion

**If pilot fails** (significant issues):
- Re-evaluate security protocols
- Consider partnerships (3rd party logistics vs in-house)
- Fallback: Branch-only model

**Approval Sought**: Authorize 5-city doorstep pilot with Rs 3 Cr investment and 6-month evaluation period (Month 6-12 of Year 1).

**Program Owner**: COO, Retail Banking (with Risk oversight)

- Review metrics dashboard (acquisition, NPA, disbursements)
- Resolve operational blockers
- Course correct on campaigns/initiatives

**Monthly**: Business Performance Review (Head of Retail + CXOs)
- Performance vs targets
- Budget vs actuals
- Strategic initiative progress
- Go/no-go on new campaigns

**Quarterly**: Board Retail Business Committee
- Strategic performance
- Phase gate reviews (proceed to next phase?)
- Risk and compliance deep dive
- Competitive intelligence

### Escalation Protocol:

**Material Issues** (NPA spike, compliance breach, fraud):
- Immediate escalation to CRO + Head of Retail
- MD & CEO briefed within 24 hours
- Board informed if impact >Rs 5 Cr or regulatory risk

**Performance Issues** (targets missed for 2+ months):
- Root cause analysis by Steering Committee
- Remediation plan within 1 week
- Monthly progress review until resolved
