**40-50% Savings for Customers**
- Kotak rate: 9.5-10.5%

- Trust advantage: Bank-grade security and compliance

**Rs 363 Cr PAT Over 3 Years**

**2.45x ROI on Rs 148 Cr Investment**
- Total 3-year investment: Rs 148 Cr
- Total 3-year PAT: Rs 363 Cr
- Payback period: 18 months
- Break-even: Month 8

**Zero Compliance Violations → Trust Leadership**

- IIFL: Banned from gold loans (October 2024)

- **Compliance is the new competitive moat**
