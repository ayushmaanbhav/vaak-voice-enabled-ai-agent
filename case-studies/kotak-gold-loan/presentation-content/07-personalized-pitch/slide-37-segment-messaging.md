## Messaging Principles

### The 4C Framework
1. **Contextual**: Message matches customer's life situation
2. **Compelling**: Clear financial benefit quantified
3. **Credible**: Bank trust + regulatory backing
4. **Convenient**: Easy next step (click, call, visit)

### Testing Strategy
- **A/B Test Variables**: Subject lines, CTA text, offer prominence
- **Success Metrics**: Open rate, click rate, conversion rate by segment
- **Iteration Cycle**: Bi-weekly optimization based on performance data

## Channel-Message Fit

| Message Type | Best Channel | Reason |
|--------------|--------------|--------|
| Savings Calculator | Email, SMS | Requires customer action/input |
| Security Emphasis | Video, Branch | Visual trust-building needed |
| Empowerment Story | Social Media, BC | Community-driven narrative |
| Speed Highlight | App Push, Instagram | Digital-native audience |
| Flexibility Offer | Regional Radio, SMS | Rural reach required |

**Key Insight**: Same segment, different channels = different message framing
