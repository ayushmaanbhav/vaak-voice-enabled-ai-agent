# Four Campaigns. Four Audiences.

## Campaign Architecture Overview

Each campaign targets a distinct segment with customized messaging, creative, channels, and conversion paths.

---

## Campaign 1: "The Smart Switch"

### Target Segment
**P1: High-Value Rational Customers**
- MSME owners, professionals with Rs 10L+ loans
- Existing Kotak Premier/Privy League customers preferred
- Age: 35-55, high financial literacy
- Current lenders: Muthoot, Manappuram, Federal Bank

### Core Message
**Show them the math. Make the switch irresistible.**

"You're paying Rs 6,250/month in unnecessary interest. Switch to Kotak and save Rs 75,000/year."

### Creative Concept
- **Visual**: Split-screen comparison - "Before/After" savings calculator
- **Tone**: Professional, data-driven, ROI-focused
- **Tagline**: "Calculate your savings. Make the smart switch."

### Channel Strategy
| Channel | Tactic | Budget Allocation |
|---------|--------|------------------|
| **RM Outreach** | Personalized email + call from relationship manager | 40% |
| **Digital Ads** | LinkedIn, Google Search (keywords: "gold loan", "business loan") | 30% |
| **Business Dailies** | Economic Times, Mint print ads with savings calculator | 20% |
| **Branch Events** | "Business Banking Day" with live savings demos | 10% |

### Conversion Path
1. **Awareness**: RM email with personalized savings calculation
2. **Interest**: Click through to dedicated landing page with live calculator
3. **Consideration**: Schedule call with RM or branch visit
4. **Conversion**: Pre-approved offer presented, loan transfer initiated

### Success Metrics
- **Target Reach**: 50,000 high-value customers
- **Conversion Rate**: 5% (2,500 customers)
- **Average Loan Size**: Rs 15L
- **CAC Target**: Rs 3,500

## Campaign 2: "Gold Deserves a Bank"

### Target Segment
**P2: Trust-Seekers (Safety-Conscious)**
- Middle-income households, age 35-60
- Women-led financial decisions in family
- Concerned about recent IIFL ban, regulatory issues
- Current lenders: Local NBFCs, smaller gold finance companies

### Core Message
**Bank vault security. RBI oversight. No regulatory worries.**

"After the IIFL ban, are you worried about your gold's safety? Kotak offers bank-level protection with Rs 3,000 cashback on transfer."

### Creative Concept
- **Visual**: Bank vault imagery, RBI logo, insurance certificate
- **Tone**: Reassuring, authoritative, protective
- **Tagline**: "Your gold deserves bank-level protection"

### Channel Strategy
| Channel | Tactic | Budget Allocation |
|---------|--------|------------------|
| **Digital Video** | YouTube pre-roll, Facebook video ads (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu) | 35% |
| **Regional TV** | 10-second spots during news/family shows | 30% |
| **Branch Activations** | "Gold Safety Awareness Week" with vault tours | 20% |
| **SMS Campaign** | Targeted SMS to identified competitor customers | 15% |

### Conversion Path
1. **Awareness**: Video ad highlighting IIFL ban, regulatory risks
2. **Interest**: Click to landing page with vault security video tour
3. **Consideration**: Call helpline or visit branch for gold transfer quote
4. **Conversion**: Branch visit, gold transferred, Rs 3,000 cashback credited

### Success Metrics
- **Target Reach**: 200,000 households
- **Conversion Rate**: 2% (4,000 customers)
- **Average Loan Size**: Rs 5L
- **CAC Target**: Rs 1,800

## Campaign 3: "Shakti Gold"

### Target Segment
**P3: Women Entrepreneurs**
- Women-owned businesses, SHG members
- Age 25-45, digitally savvy
- Seeking dignity, respect, empowerment in lending
- Current lenders: Manappuram, Muthoot, local lenders

### Core Message
**Empowerment. Dignity. Financial independence for women.**

"Your gold is your strength. Shakti Gold by Kotak - 9% exclusive rate for women + free insurance."

### Creative Concept
- **Visual**: Real women entrepreneurs, success stories, family prosperity
- **Tone**: Empowering, aspirational, community-driven
- **Tagline**: "Financial independence starts here"

### Channel Strategy
| Channel | Tactic | Budget Allocation |
|---------|--------|------------------|
| **BC Network (Sonata)** | 2.7M women micro-entrepreneurs - direct outreach | 35% |
| **Kotak 811 App** | In-app banner for women customers with gold loan affinity | 25% |
| **Social Media** | Instagram/Facebook - women influencer partnerships | 20% |
| **SHG Meetings** | Partnership with Sonata Finance for group presentations | 20% |

### Conversion Path
1. **Awareness**: BC agent introduces Shakti Gold at SHG meeting
2. **Interest**: Group demo of app-based loan application
3. **Consideration**: Pre-approval on app, doorstep gold collection scheduled
4. **Conversion**: Gold collected, loan disbursed, insurance activated

### Success Metrics
- **Target Reach**: 100,000 women entrepreneurs
- **Conversion Rate**: 3% (3,000 customers)
- **Average Loan Size**: Rs 3L
- **CAC Target**: Rs 1,200

## Campaign 4: "Gold in Clicks"

### Target Segment
**P4: Young Professionals (Digital-First)**
- Age 25-35, salaried, tech-savvy
- Existing Kotak 811 users preferred
- Hate branch visits, want instant digital experience
- Current lenders: Federal Bank, HDFC, digital-first lenders

### Core Message
**Instant. Digital. Zero hassle.**

"Turn idle gold into instant opportunity. Loan approved in 15 clicks, money in 2 hours. No branch visit needed."

### Creative Concept
- **Visual**: Mobile app UI, speed animations, millennial lifestyle
- **Tone**: Fast-paced, modern, tech-forward
- **Tagline**: "Gold loan in clicks"

### Channel Strategy
| Channel | Tactic | Budget Allocation |
|---------|--------|------------------|
| **Kotak 811 App** | Push notifications to 20M+ users with gold loan affinity | 40% |
| **Instagram/YouTube** | Influencer partnerships, short-form video content | 30% |
| **Google/Facebook Ads** | Retargeting users who searched "instant gold loan" | 20% |
| **Referral Program** | Rs 500 bonus for customer + referee | 10% |

### Conversion Path
1. **Awareness**: App push notification "Pre-approved for Rs 2L gold loan"
2. **Interest**: Click through to in-app loan journey
3. **Consideration**: Live chat support, instant pre-approval shown
4. **Conversion**: Doorstep gold collection scheduled, loan disbursed to 811 account

### Success Metrics
- **Target Reach**: 500,000 Kotak 811 users
- **Conversion Rate**: 1% (5,000 customers)
- **Average Loan Size**: Rs 2L
- **CAC Target**: Rs 800

## Integrated Campaign Calendar

### Phase 1: Launch (Month 1-2)
- **Campaign 2 ("Gold Deserves a Bank")**: Capitalize on IIFL ban news cycle
- Focus on safety messaging, vault security, regulatory trust

### Phase 2: Expansion (Month 3-4)
- **Campaign 1 ("The Smart Switch")**: Target high-value customers with savings pitch
- **Campaign 4 ("Gold in Clicks")**: Launch digital-first campaign on Kotak 811

### Phase 3: Deepening (Month 5-6)
- **Campaign 3 ("Shakti Gold")**: Women's Day tie-in, BC network activation
- Festival season timing (Navratri, Diwali)

### Phase 4: Optimization (Month 7-12)
- A/B testing across all campaigns
- Reallocate budget to highest-performing segment/channel combinations
- Introduce referral incentives for converted customers

---

## Cross-Campaign Synergies

### Unified Branding Elements
- **Color Palette**: Kotak Red, Blue, Gold across all campaigns
- **Logo Treatment**: "Kotak Gold Loan" master brand + campaign sub-brands
- **Music**: Same audio signature across TV/video ads

### Shared Infrastructure
- **Landing Page Template**: Segment-specific customization on common CMS
- **CRM Integration**: All campaigns feed into unified lead management system
- **Helpline**: Single 1800 number with IVR routing by campaign code

### Measurement Framework
- **Weekly Dashboards**: Reach, engagement, conversion by campaign
- **Monthly Reviews**: Budget reallocation based on CAC and conversion efficiency
- **Quarterly Deep Dive**: Customer quality (LTV, default rate) by campaign source
