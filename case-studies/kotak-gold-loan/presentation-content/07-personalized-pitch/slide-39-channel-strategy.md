## Digital Channels - Infrastructure & Reach

### 1. Kotak 811 App
**Reach**: 20M+ active users | **Penetration**: 15M+ in Tier 1/2 cities

**Capabilities**:
- In-app gold loan journey (KYC pre-populated)
- Instant pre-approval notifications
- Live gold rate calculator
- Doorstep collection booking
- Loan status tracking

**Use Cases**:
- **Push Notifications**: Targeted to users with gold loan affinity (competitor payment patterns detected)
- **Banner Ads**: Home screen placement for high-propensity users (75+ score)
- **In-App Chat**: Real-time query resolution, lead capture

**Expected Performance**:
- **Reach**: 500,000 targeted users
- **Click Rate**: 8%
- **Conversion Rate**: 1%
- **Customers Acquired**: 5,000
- **CAC**: Rs 600

---

### 2. SMS & Push Notifications
**Reach**: 15M+ Kotak customers | **Open Rate**: 95% (SMS), 60% (Push)

**Segmentation Logic**:
- **High Propensity (75+)**: Weekly personalized offers
- **Medium Propensity (50-74)**: Bi-weekly rate alerts
- **Low Propensity (<50)**: Monthly brand awareness

**Message Formats**:
```
HIGH PROPENSITY:
"Rajesh, reduce your Rs 25L gold loan from 12% to 9.5%. Save Rs 6,250/month.
Pre-approved. Apply: kotak.com/gl?id=R1234"

MEDIUM PROPENSITY:
"Kotak Gold Loan now at 9% for women. Zero processing fee.
Check eligibility: kotak.com/shakti"

LOW PROPENSITY:
"Turn idle gold into opportunity. Kotak Gold Loan starting 8.5%.
Learn more: kotak.com/goldloan"
```

**Expected Performance**:
- **Reach**: 1M targeted customers
- **Click Rate**: 12% (SMS), 5% (Push)
- **Conversion Rate**: 0.8%
- **Customers Acquired**: 8,000
- **CAC**: Rs 400

## Offline Channels - Physical Reach & Trust

### 5. Relationship Manager (RM) Outreach
**Coverage**: 2,500 RMs across 1,600+ branches | **High-Touch Model**

**Target Allocation**:
- Each RM assigned 50 high-propensity leads/month
- Focus on existing Kotak Premier/Privy League customers with competitor gold loans

**RM Playbook**:
1. **Personalized Email**: Sent from RM's email with savings calculation
2. **Follow-Up Call**: Within 48 hours of email send
3. **Branch Appointment**: Scheduled for loan discussion
4. **Pre-Approved Offer**: Presented at branch with one-page comparison sheet

**RM Incentives**:
- Rs 1,500 per gold loan disbursed (>Rs 10L)
- Rs 500 per gold loan disbursed (<Rs 10L)
- Quarterly bonus for top performers

**Expected Performance**:
- **Reach**: 50,000 customers
- **Appointment Rate**: 20%
- **Conversion Rate**: 25% (of appointments)
- **Customers Acquired**: 2,500
- **CAC**: Rs 3,500

## Channel Efficiency Analysis

### CAC Comparison by Channel

```
Digital Channels (Avg CAC: Rs 850)
├── SMS/Push:        Rs 400 ✓ Most efficient
├── Kotak 811 App:   Rs 600 ✓ High conversion
├── Email:           Rs 800
└── Social Media:    Rs 1,200

Offline Channels (Avg CAC: Rs 1,900)
├── BC Network:      Rs 1,000 ✓ Best offline channel
├── Regional Media:  Rs 1,500
├── Branch:          Rs 2,000
└── RM Outreach:     Rs 3,500 (but highest loan size)
```

**Key Insights**:
- Digital channels deliver 55% lower CAC than offline
- But offline channels convert 2x higher loan amounts
- Blended CAC target: Rs 1,500 (60% digital, 40% offline)

## Omnichannel Integration Strategy

### Seamless Customer Journey

**Scenario 1: Digital-First Customer**
1. Receives SMS pre-approval → Clicks link
2. Lands on mobile-optimized page → Starts application
3. Stuck on document upload → Initiates chat
4. Chat agent schedules branch appointment
5. Visits branch → Loan disbursed

**Scenario 2: Branch-First Customer**
1. Walks into branch with inquiry
2. Branch executive creates lead in CRM
3. Customer receives SMS with savings calculator link
4. Completes application online
5. Doorstep gold collection scheduled via app

**Scenario 3: BC-Enabled Customer**
1. BC agent presents Shakti Gold at SHG meeting
2. Group members download Kotak 811 app
3. BC assists with KYC, gold collection
4. Loan approved, disbursed to 811 account
5. Customer refers friend → Referral bonus

### Real-Time Tracking Metrics
- **Reach**: Total customers exposed by channel
- **Engagement**: Click rate, appointment rate, app downloads
- **Conversion**: Loan applications, disbursals
- **Cost**: CAC by channel, blended CAC
- **Quality**: Loan size, default rate, LTV by acquisition channel

### Monthly Optimization Actions
- Shift budget from underperforming channels (CAC >Rs 2,500)
- Double down on high-performing channels (CAC <Rs 1,000)
- Test new channel experiments (e.g., WhatsApp Business, Google Maps ads)
- Refine audience targeting based on conversion patterns
