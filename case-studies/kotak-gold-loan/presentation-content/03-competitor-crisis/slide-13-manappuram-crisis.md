### RBI Actions: Unprecedented Enforcement

#### **October 2024: The Asirvad Ban**
**First NBFC Subsidiary Business Ban in Recent History**

- **Action**: RBI **BANS** Asirvad Microfinance from sanctioning/disbursing loans
- **Reasons**:
  - Pricing policy violations
  - Aggressive collection malpractices
  - Supervisory concerns on loan practices
- **Impact**:
  - Stock fell **14%** in single trading session
  - Business completely halted
  - Regulatory scrutiny on parent company intensified
  - Customer confidence shattered

**Significance**: Shows RBI's willingness to use extreme measures. No NBFC is too big to ban.

#### **Previous RBI Penalties**

| Date | Amount | Violation | Lesson |
|------|--------|-----------|--------|
