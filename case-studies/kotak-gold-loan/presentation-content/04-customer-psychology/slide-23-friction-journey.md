## The Bridge Loan: Financial Engineering as Competitive Moat

### How It Works

**Customer Scenario:**
- Existing loan with Muthoot: ₹5,00,000 @ 18%
- Gold value: ₹10,00,000
- Customer wants to switch to Kotak @ 10%

**Traditional Problem:**
> Customer needs ₹5L cash to close Muthoot loan before pledging to Kotak

**Kotak Bridge Loan Solution:**

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  STEP 1: Kotak Pre-Approves New Loan             │
│  Amount: ₹5,00,000 @ 10% (against gold)          │
│                                                   │
│  STEP 2: Kotak Issues Bridge Loan                │
│  Amount: ₹5,00,000 (exact closure amount)        │
│  Tenure: 3 days (until gold is transferred)      │
│  Collateral: Customer's signature + gold rights  │
│  Interest: ₹0 (absorbed by Kotak)                │
│                                                   │
│  STEP 3: Kotak Pays Muthoot Directly             │
│  Bank transfer: ₹5,00,000 to Muthoot account     │
│  Authorization: Customer signs NACH mandate       │
│  Closure: Muthoot releases gold to Kotak         │
│                                                   │
│  STEP 4: Bridge Loan Auto-Closes                 │
│  Gold arrives at Kotak vault                     │
│  Bridge loan converts to regular gold loan       │
│  Customer's new ₹5L Kotak loan starts @ 10%      │
│  Net customer cash flow: ₹0                      │
│                                                   │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Bridge Loan Economics (Kotak Perspective)

**Cost to Kotak:**
- 3-day interest on ₹5L @ 10% = ₹410
- Insurance for transit = ₹500
- Logistics coordination = ₹1,000
- **Total Cost: ₹1,910 per customer**

**Benefit to Kotak:**
- Acquire ₹5L loan @ 10% = ₹50,000 annual revenue
- Customer lifetime value (assuming 3-year tenure) = ₹1,50,000
- Cross-sell potential (savings account, cards, insurance) = ₹30,000
- **Total Benefit: ₹1,80,000**

**ROI: 94x on acquisition cost**

## Research Foundation

**Process Mapping:**
- Ethnographic research: Shadowed 15 customers through traditional switching
- Recorded every step, every delay, every moment of anxiety
- Identified 23 distinct friction points in old journey
- Kotak process eliminated 19 of them

**Success Rate Data:**
- Traditional switching: Surveys of 500 customers who ATTEMPTED to switch
- 42% completed switch
- 35% gave up at fund arrangement stage
- 15% gave up due to transit fear
- 8% gave up due to valuation disappointment

**Customer Sentiment:**
- Pre-switch anxiety score: 7.8/10 (traditional) vs. 2.1/10 (Kotak)
- Post-switch satisfaction: 6.2/10 (traditional) vs. 9.1/10 (Kotak)
- Net Promoter Score: 32 (traditional) vs. 78 (Kotak)
