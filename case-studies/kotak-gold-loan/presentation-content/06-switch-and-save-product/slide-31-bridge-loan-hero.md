## Three Friction Points SOLVED

### 1. Cash Flow Gap
**PROBLEM**: "I need Rs 5 lakh to close my Muthoot loan, but I don't have it lying around"
- Can't borrow from family (embarrassing)
- Credit card limit insufficient
- Don't want to sell investments

**SOLUTION**: Kotak provides Rs 5L bridge loan at 0% for 7 days
- Closes Muthoot loan directly
- Customer pays ZERO
- Automatically replaced by gold loan within 3 days

---

### 2. Gold in Transit Anxiety
**PROBLEM**: "What if my Rs 5 lakh worth of gold gets lost/stolen while moving from Muthoot to Kotak?"
- Personal liability during transit
- No insurance coverage
- Anxiety about handling valuables

**SOLUTION**: Kotak-insured professional transit service
- Full insurance coverage (Rs 100-200 cost absorbed by Kotak)
- Professional handling by trained personnel
- Zero customer liability
- Peace of mind

---

### 3. Multiple Branch Visits & Timing Coordination
**PROBLEM**: "I need to visit Muthoot twice, Kotak twice, coordinate closures, take time off work..."
- 4-6 branch visits over 10-14 days
- Complex timing coordination
- Work leave required
- High effort barrier

**SOLUTION**: Streamlined 3-day process with pre-approval
- Pre-approved for existing Kotak customers
- Kotak handles NBFC closure directly
- Professional transit service
- 2 visits max: Gold redemption + Gold pledging
- Everything else handled by Kotak backend
