## FAQs: Customer Concerns Addressed

### "What if the gold value is less than my NBFC loan amount?"
**ANSWER**: We verify expected gold value before sanctioning bridge loan. If there's a shortfall (rare), you have two options:
1. Pay the difference (small amount, e.g., Rs 10K-20K on Rs 5L loan)
2. Cancel the switch - bridge loan is reversed, no penalty (extremely rare scenario)

**Reality**: 95%+ of cases, gold value exceeds NBFC loan (gold prices rise over time)

---

### "What happens if I don't bring the gold within 7 days?"
**ANSWER**: Bridge loan has 7-day interest-free period. If you don't complete by Day 7:
- Day 8-14: Interest applies at normal gold loan rate (9.5-10.5%)
- Day 15: Amount auto-debited from Kotak account (you signed authorization)

**Reality**: 95%+ complete within 3 days. Kotak provides proactive reminders + support.

---

### "Can I back out after accepting the bridge loan?"
**ANSWER**: Yes, until Day 1 disbursement. After disbursement:
- You must complete the switch OR
- Repay bridge loan within 7 days (no interest if within 7 days)
- Auto-debit from Kotak account applies if not repaid

**Reality**: <2% cancellation rate after bridge loan acceptance (high intent customers)

---

### "Is the gold safe during transit?"
**ANSWER**: Absolutely. Kotak's insured transit service includes:
- Full value insurance (Rs 3L-1Cr coverage)
- Professional logistics partner (trained personnel)
- Real-time GPS tracking
- Zero customer liability
- Cost absorbed by Kotak (Rs 100-200)

**Reality**: 99.9%+ transit success rate. Zero customer loss incidents.

---

### "How do I know my NBFC loan is really closed?"
**ANSWER**: You receive:
1. NBFC closure certificate (same day)
2. SMS from NBFC confirming loan closed
3. Gold release authorization from NBFC
4. Kotak confirmation with payment reference

**Reality**: Direct bank-to-bank payment eliminates errors. 100% transparency.
