# Switch Assist: White-Glove Service

## Premium Concierge Experience for Gold Loan Switching

---

## Service Overview

### What is Switch Assist?

**Definition**: A comprehensive white-glove service that handles every aspect of switching from an NBFC/competitor bank gold loan to Kotak, eliminating all customer friction and anxiety.

**Philosophy**: Treat gold loan switching like a premium banking service, not a commoditized transaction. Invest in high-touch support that creates lifelong customers, not one-time switchers.

**Target Audience**: Existing Kotak customers (Savings/Current account, Priority/Privy League) with active gold loans at NBFCs/competitor banks, loan size Rs 3L+.

---

## Service Components: The 5 Pillars

### 1. Instant Pre-Approval
**Removes Uncertainty Anxiety**

#### What We Do
- Automated pre-approval within 30 seconds to 2 minutes
- Real-time decisioning based on existing Kotak relationship
- Clear, upfront offer with exact rate, amount, and bridge loan eligibility
- No paperwork required for pre-approval stage

#### Customer Benefit
- **Certainty**: Know you're approved before starting
- **Transparency**: See exact rate and savings upfront
- **Speed**: No waiting days for loan approval
- **Confidence**: Start the switch with zero uncertainty

#### Cost to Kotak
- **System Cost**: Minimal (automated engine)
- **Marginal Cost**: Rs 0 per pre-approval
- **Infrastructure**: One-time investment in decisioning engine

#### Success Metrics
- 85-90% pre-approval rate (existing customers)
- <2 minutes average pre-approval time
- 70-80% pre-approval to bridge loan acceptance conversion

---

### 2. 7-Day Zero-Interest Bridge Financing
**Removes Cash Flow Gap**

#### What We Do
- Provide interest-free bridge loan to close existing NBFC loan
- Amount: Exact closure amount (Rs 3L-1Cr)
- Direct payment to NBFC (customer doesn't handle funds)
- Automatic replacement with gold loan upon pledge

#### Customer Benefit
- **Zero Out-of-Pocket**: No need to arrange Rs 3-10L upfront
- **No Borrowing**: No embarrassing family loans or expensive credit cards
- **Direct Closure**: Kotak pays NBFC directly, customer just redeems gold
- **Seamless**: Bridge loan disappears when gold loan activates

#### Cost to Kotak
- **Float Cost**: ~Rs 200 per Rs 5L bridge (7-day opportunity cost)
- **Risk Cost**: Minimal (existing customer + gold security)
- **Total**: Rs 200-300 per switch

#### Success Metrics
- 90-95% bridge loan completion rate (minimal defaults)
- 75-85% overall switch success (vs 40-50% without bridge)
- 3 days average time to completion (vs 10-14 days without)

---

### 3. Insured Transit Service
**Removes Safety Anxiety**

#### What We Do
- Professional logistics partner with full value insurance
- Real-time GPS tracking during gold movement
- Trained personnel for gold handling
- Door-to-door service (NBFC branch → Kotak branch)
- Cost absorbed by Kotak (Rs 100-200 per transit)

#### Customer Benefit
- **Zero Liability**: Customer not responsible for gold during transit
- **Peace of Mind**: Rs 3L-1Cr fully insured
- **Professional Handling**: No personal risk of carrying valuables
- **Tracking**: Know exactly where gold is at all times

#### Cost to Kotak
- **Insurance Premium**: Rs 50-100 per transit
- **Logistics Fee**: Rs 50-100 per transit
- **Total**: Rs 100-200 per switch

#### Success Metrics
- 99.9%+ successful transits (zero loss incidents)
- 80%+ customers opt for insured service (vs personal transit)
- NPS boost of +20 points from this service alone

---

### 4. Dedicated Switch Assist Team
**Removes Coordination Hassle**

#### What We Do
- Assign single point of contact for entire switching journey
- Proactive communication (SMS, WhatsApp, calls at each milestone)
- Coordinate NBFC closure, gold redemption, Kotak pledge
- Handhold through 3-day process with daily check-ins
- Available 6 days/week, 9 AM - 7 PM

#### Customer Benefit
- **Single Contact**: No calling multiple departments
- **Proactive Updates**: Don't have to chase for status
- **Expert Guidance**: Know exactly what to do at each step
- **Stress-Free**: Someone else manages the timeline and coordination

#### Cost to Kotak
- **Staff Time**: ~2 hours per switch (across 3 days)
- **Loaded Cost**: Rs 100-150 per switch
- **Scalability**: 1 team member handles 10-15 switches concurrently

#### Success Metrics
- 95%+ customer satisfaction with support (CSAT score)
- <2 hour response time for customer queries
- 90%+ complete within 3 days (proactive coordination works)

- **Fast Processing**: Pre-verified customer data speeds approval

#### Cost to Kotak

## Customer Journey with Switch Assist

### Touchpoints: From Interest to Advocacy

#### Pre-Switch (Day -7 to Day 0)
- **Outreach**: Targeted SMS/WhatsApp/App notification to existing customers
- **Pre-Approval**: Instant pre-approval issued within 2 minutes
- **Education**: Video/FAQ explaining bridge loan + 3-day process
- **Intent Capture**: "Get Started" CTA → bridge loan acceptance

**Success Rate**: 30-40% of targeted customers show interest

---

#### Switch Execution (Day 0 to Day 3)
- **Day 0 Evening**: Welcome SMS + Switch Assist team introduction
  - Assigned dedicated support contact (name, phone, WhatsApp)
  - What to expect: Step-by-step 3-day timeline

- **Day 1 Morning**: Bridge loan sanction + NBFC closure
  - SMS: "Bridge loan approved, closing your Muthoot loan today"
  - WhatsApp: Payment reference + timeline for gold redemption
  - Call: Switch Assist team confirms NBFC branch details

- **Day 1 Afternoon**: NBFC loan closed
  - SMS: "Your Muthoot loan is closed. Visit branch tomorrow to redeem gold."
  - WhatsApp: NBFC closure certificate + what to bring for redemption
  - Transit service scheduling (if opted)

- **Day 2 Morning**: Gold redemption preparation
  - SMS reminder: "Today is gold redemption day! Branch address: [NBFC branch]"
  - WhatsApp: Checklist (ID proof, loan closure certificate)
  - Transit partner contact (if using insured service)

- **Day 2 Afternoon**: Gold redeemed + transit initiated
  - Customer confirms gold received from NBFC
  - Transit partner picks up gold (if using service)
  - Real-time GPS tracking link sent via WhatsApp
  - SMS: "Gold in transit to Kotak branch, ETA: [time]"

- **Day 3 Morning**: Gold pledge at Kotak
  - SMS reminder: "Gold pledge appointment at [Kotak branch] today at [time]"
  - Fast-track processing at branch (pre-booked slot)
  - Purity test + valuation completed

- **Day 3 Afternoon**: Gold loan active
  - Bridge loan auto-replaced with gold loan
  - SMS: "Congratulations! Your Kotak Gold Loan is active at 10% p.a. Saving Rs 2,500/month!"
  - WhatsApp: Loan details, repayment schedule, savings calculator
  - Email: Loan agreement + gold pledge receipt

**Success Rate**: 75-85% completion from bridge loan acceptance

---

#### Post-Switch (Day 4 to Day 30)
- **Day 4-7**: Cashback credited
  - SMS: "Cashback Rs 2,000 credited to your account. Thank you for choosing Kotak!"
  - App notification: "Check your new gold loan details in Kotak app"

- **Week 2**: First interest debit
  - SMS: "First interest payment of Rs 4,167 debited. You're saving Rs 2,500/month vs NBFC!"
  - WhatsApp: Savings tracker + cumulative savings visualization

- **Month 1**: Satisfaction survey + cross-sell
  - NPS survey: "Rate your switching experience (0-10)"
  - If NPS 9-10: "Refer a friend and earn Rs 1,000"
  - Cross-sell offer: "Protect your gold loan with insurance for Rs 50/month"

- **Month 3-6**: Relationship deepening
  - Invite to Priority Banking (if not already)
  - Wealth management consultation offer
  - Business loan/working capital pre-approval (for MSME customers)

**Advocacy Rate**: 40% refer others (NPS 70+)
