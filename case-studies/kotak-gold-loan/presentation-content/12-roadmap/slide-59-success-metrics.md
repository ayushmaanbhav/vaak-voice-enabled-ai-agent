## Review Cadence & Governance

### Weekly Operational Review
**Participants**: Product Manager, Operations Head, Tech Lead
**Focus**: Tactical execution and firefighting

**Metrics Reviewed**:
- Daily disbursements (volume and value)
- Application funnel (leads, conversions, drop-offs)
- NPA movement (new defaults, recoveries)
- System uptime and performance
- Customer complaints and escalations

**Format**: 30-minute standup with dashboard review
**Action Items**: Captured in weekly tracker with owners and deadlines

---

### Monthly Business Review
**Participants**: Head of Retail Banking, Product Manager, Operations, Marketing, Risk
**Focus**: Business performance against targets

**Metrics Reviewed**:
- All acquisition metrics (customers, CAC, conversion)
- All portfolio metrics (AUM, yield, ticket size)
- NPS and customer satisfaction scores
- Marketing campaign effectiveness
- Budget vs actuals

**Format**: 90-minute deep dive with presentations
**Decisions**: Go/no-go on campaigns, budget adjustments, target revisions

---

### Monthly Compliance Review
**Participants**: Chief Risk Officer, Compliance Head, Product Manager, Operations
**Focus**: Risk and regulatory adherence

**Metrics Reviewed**:
- All quality metrics (NPA, LTV compliance, audit findings)
- Fraud incidents and patterns
- Regulatory correspondence and queries
- Internal audit findings and remediation status
- RBI return filings and accuracy

**Format**: 60-minute review with deep dive on any red flags
**Escalation**: Material issues flagged to MD & CEO within 24 hours

---

### Quarterly Board Review
**Participants**: Board Retail Business Committee, MD & CEO, CXOs
**Focus**: Strategic progress and governance

**Metrics Reviewed**:
- North Star metric (AUM growth)
- Top 3 metrics per category (acquisition, portfolio, quality, experience, financial)
- Phase gate progress (on track for next phase?)
- Strategic initiatives (AI rollout, branch expansion, product launches)
- Competitive intelligence and market share

**Format**: 30-minute board paper + 60-minute discussion
**Decisions**: Strategic pivots, major investments, risk appetite adjustments

**Board Paper Sections**:
1. Executive Summary (1 page)
2. Performance Dashboard (2 pages)
3. Strategic Initiatives Update (2 pages)
4. Risk & Compliance Report (1 page)
5. Recommendations & Approvals Sought (1 page)
