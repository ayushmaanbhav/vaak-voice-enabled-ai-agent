## The Ask in Plain English

**What We're Asking For:**
- Rs 53 Cr Year 1 (3.2% of annual IT budget)
- Rs 148 Cr over 3 years (3% of 3-year IT budget)

**What Kotak Gets:**
- Rs 363 Cr PAT in 3 years (2.45x ROI)
- 425,000 new customers (gold loan + cross-sell potential)
- Rs 8,500 Cr AUM (strategic lending book)
- Competitive moat in secured lending
- AI/tech capabilities reusable across Kotak

**Risk Level:**
- MINIMAL relative to Kotak's capacity
- LOW absolute risk (gold secured, 0.3% NPA)
- PHASED investment (can pause if needed)
- PROVEN market (Rs 6.4 lakh Cr industry)
