## Investment Philosophy

- **Critical** (Rs 30 Cr): Core capabilities needed for market entry
- **Experimental** (Rs 19.5 Cr): Innovation with measurable pilots
- **Buffer** (Rs 3.5 Cr): Risk mitigation for unknowns

- 27% allocated to AI/Technology - future-proofing the business

- 19% to Compliance & Security - regulatory excellence from day one
