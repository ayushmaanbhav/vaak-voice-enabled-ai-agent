   - Muthoot Finance: ₹82,000 crore

3. **Total:** ₹7.1 lakh crore

### Growth Drivers
1. **Increasing Gold Prices:** Gold price appreciation drives loan eligibility
2. **Financial Inclusion:** Tier 2/3 cities accessing organized credit
3. **MSME Demand:** Business working capital needs
4. **Agricultural Sector:** Seasonal financing requirements
5. **Digital Adoption:** Online gold loans expanding reach

### Competitive Growth Rates
- **Banks:** 71.3% YoY (FY25) - highest growth
- **NBFCs:** 8.2% YoY (FY25) - slowing
- **Overall Market:** 14.85% CAGR (projected)
