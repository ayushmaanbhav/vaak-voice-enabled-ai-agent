## Backup Data

### Why Monetization Is So Low

#### Cultural Factors
1. **Emotional Attachment:** Gold = family heirloom, wedding gift, ancestral wealth
2. **Social Status:** Gold ownership = prestige marker
3. **Festival/Wedding Reserve:** Kept for future ceremonies
4. **Distrust of Institutions:** Historical preference for physical asset control

#### Structural Barriers
1. **Awareness Gap:** 47% don't know about gold loan products (Tier 2/3 cities)
2. **Process Friction:** Branch visit requirement, documentation
3. **Purity Concerns:** Fear of gold damage during appraisal
4. **Return Uncertainty:** Unclear auction processes if default

#### Market Evolution
**Barriers Breaking Down:**
- Digital gold loans reducing process friction
- Doorstep collection building trust
- Transparent purity testing (XRF machines visible to customer)
- Clear LTV and auction terms
- Bank entry bringing institutional trust

### Organized vs Unorganized: Why Customers Choose Each

#### Why Customers Use Unorganized Sector
| Reason | % of Users | Organized Solution |
|--------|------------|-------------------|
| Faster disbursement | 34% | Digital gold loans (30 min) |
| No documentation | 28% | Minimal doc + Aadhaar-based |
| Known lender (trust) | 22% | Bank brand trust |
| Privacy concerns | 16% | Discrete branch/doorstep service |

#### Migration Triggers to Organized Sector
1. **Rate Shock:** Discovering they pay 40-50% vs bank 10-12%
2. **Gold Safety:** Pawnbroker shop security concerns
3. **Legal Issues:** Unorganized lender harassment
4. **Transparency:** Clear terms, digital tracking
5. **Top-up Needs:** Organized sector offers better renewal terms
