## 10-Point Prevention Framework

### System-Enforced Safeguards

1. **Zero cash disbursement** (system-enforced, no override)
   - 100% digital disbursement to customer account
   - Prevents cash handling fraud, regulatory violations

2. **Real-time LTV monitoring with auto-alerts**
   - Daily gold price updates, automatic customer alerts at 80% LTV
   - SMS/email notifications, self-service top-up portal

3. **BIS-certified XRF at 100% gold loan branches**
   - Q2 2025: 100% coverage across 250 branches
   - Annual re-certification, external quality audits

4. **Customer presence verification (video recorded)**
   - Mandatory video recording of gold handover/valuation
   - 7-year retention, blockchain timestamps for authenticity

5. **Centralized auction with transparency portal**
   - Customer portal to track auction process, bid details
   - Video recording of auctions, public price transparency

6. **Mandatory 90-day NPA classification (no exceptions)**
   - System-enforced, no manual overrides, automatic provisioning
   - Prevents evergreening, ensures clean books

7. **Fresh appraisal for all renewals/top-ups**
   - No auto-renewals, every transaction re-appraised
   - Prevents purity degradation, LTV creep

8. **Monthly compliance reporting to Risk Committee**
   - 20+ metrics dashboard, trend analysis, exception reports
   - Independent audit validation

9. **Quarterly external compliance audit**
   - Third-party auditor (Big 4), surprise branch inspections
   - Public scorecard for customer transparency

10. **Annual RBI-simulated inspection**
    - Internal team simulates RBI inspection, identifies gaps
    - Remediation plan 90 days before actual inspection window
