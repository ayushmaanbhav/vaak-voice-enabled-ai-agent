## Decision Rules for Doorstep Service

### Standard Policy
**Minimum Ticket Size:** Rs 50,000
- Below Rs 50K â†’ Incentivize branch/digital channels
- Rs 50K-1L â†’ Available on request, promotional pricing
- Above Rs 1L â†’ Proactively offered as standard option
- Above Rs 2L â†’ White-glove service with relationship manager

### Exceptions (Strategic Customers)
Doorstep service can be offered below Rs 50K threshold for:
- **High-lifetime-value customers** (Privy League/811 Super)
- **First-time acquisition** from competitor (customer acquisition subsidy)
- **Referral customers** (one-time courtesy)
- **Repeat customers** with good payment history

## Break-Even Economics

### Revenue vs. Cost Analysis
**Assumptions:**
- Interest rate: 10% per annum
- Loan tenure: 12 months
- Processing fee: 1% of loan amount

| Loan Amount | Annual Interest | Processing Fee | Total Revenue | Cost | Net Margin |
|-------------|----------------|----------------|---------------|------|------------|
| Rs 25,000 | Rs 2,500 | Rs 250 | Rs 2,750 | Rs 850 | Rs 1,900 (69%) |
| Rs 50,000 | Rs 5,000 | Rs 500 | Rs 5,500 | Rs 850 | Rs 4,650 (85%) |
| Rs 1,00,000 | Rs 10,000 | Rs 1,000 | Rs 11,000 | Rs 850 | Rs 10,150 (92%) |
| Rs 2,00,000 | Rs 20,000 | Rs 2,000 | Rs 22,000 | Rs 850 | Rs 21,150 (96%) |

### The Viability Question
> **Why not offer doorstep for Rs 25K if margin is still 69%?**

**Answer:** Customer perception and competitive positioning
- High service cost signals premium positioning
- Need to maintain economics at scale
- Avoid cannibalizing profitable branch channel
- Reserve doorstep as differentiator for higher-value segments

## Sweet Spot Analysis

### Optimal Loan Size: Rs 1L - Rs 3L
**Reasons:**
1. **Cost efficiency:** <1% of loan amount
2. **Customer willingness to pay:** Convenience premium accepted
3. **Risk management:** Lower ticket size limits transit risk
4. **Market alignment:** Average gold loan in Tier 1 cities is Rs 1.2L

## Channel Incentive Structure

### Steering Customers to Right Channel

| Channel | Minimum Ticket | Incentive/Disincentive |
|---------|---------------|------------------------|
| **Digital (App)** | No minimum | 0.25% rate discount |
| **Branch** | No minimum | Standard rate |
| **Doorstep** | Rs 50K | No discount (convenience premium) |

### Revenue Optimization
- Push high-volume, low-ticket loans to digital/branch
- Reserve doorstep for high-value, convenience-seeking customers
- Use doorstep as acquisition tool for competitor switchers

## Risk Guardrails

### Maximum Exposure per Doorstep Transaction
- **Single loan cap:** Rs 5L (higher amounts require branch visit)
- **Daily cap per executive:** Rs 10L in gold value
- **Reason:** Limit transit risk and insurance exposure

### Quality Control
- Random audits of 10% doorstep transactions
- Customer feedback mandatory within 24 hours
- Executive performance tracked on disbursement time, accuracy, NPS

---

**Decision:** Proceed with Rs 50K minimum, with exceptions for strategic customers
