# 5-City Doorstep Pilot Plan

## Cities Selected for Pilot

### Selection Criteria
1. **Tier 1 cities** with high gold loan demand
2. **Strong Kotak branch presence**
3. **Geographic diversity** (North/South/West representation)
4. **High average ticket size** in existing gold loan portfolio
5. **Tech-savvy customer base** for app adoption

## Pilot Parameters

### Scale and Scope

| Parameter | Target | Rationale |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **Duration** | 6 months | Sufficient for seasonal variation + iterations |
| **Target Loans** | 500 total | 100/month avg, statistically significant sample |
| **Minimum Ticket Size** | Rs 50,000 | Economic viability threshold |
| **Field Executives** | 10 (2 per city) | Handle 50 loans/month with buffer |
| **Vehicles** | 10 (2 per city) | One backup per city for redundancy |
| **XRF Devices** | 10 (2 per city) | Portable, insured, calibrated quarterly |
| **Operating Hours** | 9 AM - 7 PM | Extended hours vs. branch (9-5) |
| **Service Area** | 25 km radius from hub branch | Manageable travel time <1 hour |

## Pilot Timeline

### Month 1: Setup & Training
- Week 1-2: Recruit field executives
- Week 3: Technology platform setup
- Week 4: Training program (gold appraisal, customer service, compliance)

### Month 2: Soft Launch
- Invite-only for Privy League customers
- 20 transactions target
- Focus on process refinement

### Month 3-4: Full Launch
- Open to all eligible customers (Rs 50K+ loans)
- Marketing campaign activated
- 50-60 transactions/month target

### Month 5-6: Optimization & Scale Decision
- Data analysis and performance review
- Customer feedback integration
- Go/No-Go decision for wider rollout

## Risk Mitigation

### Operational Risks
- **Gold transit loss:** Insured up to Rs 50L per trip, GPS tracking mandatory
- **Executive fraud:** Background verification, dual custody for gold >Rs 2L
- **Device failure:** Backup XRF device at hub branch
- **Customer complaints:** 24/7 helpline, escalation to branch manager

### Market Risks
- **Low adoption:** Aggressive marketing, referral bonuses (Rs 1,000 per referred customer)
- **Competitor response:** Price matching not recommended; compete on trust + service quality
- **Regulatory change:** Compliance team on standby for RBI circular monitoring
