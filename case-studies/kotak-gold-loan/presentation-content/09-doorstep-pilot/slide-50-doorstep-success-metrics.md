# Doorstep Pilot: Success Criteria

## Primary Metrics (Go/No-Go Decision Makers)

### 1. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

| Metric | Target | Acceptable Range | Red Flag |
|--------|--------|-----------------|----------|
| **CAC per Customer** | <Rs 10,000 | Rs 8,000-12,000 | >Rs 15,000 |

**Calculation:**
```
CAC = (Marketing Spend + Sales Expense + Doorstep Service Cost) / # of New Customers Acquired
```

**Why this matters:**
- Competitor CAC for gold loan: Rs 8,000-12,000
- Lifetime value of gold loan customer: Rs 25,000-30,000 (assuming 3-year relationship)
- CAC should be <33% of LTV for healthy unit economics

### 2. Net Promoter Score (NPS)

| Metric | Target | Acceptable Range | Red Flag |
|--------|--------|-----------------|----------|
| **NPS (out of 5)** | >4.0 | 3.8-4.2 | <3.5 |

**Measurement:**
- Survey sent within 24 hours of disbursement
- Question: "How likely are you to recommend Kotak doorstep gold loan to friends/family?" (1-5 scale)
- Mandatory response for service improvement

**Components Tracked:**
- **Convenience:** Time saved vs. branch visit
- **Trust:** Executive professionalism, gold handling
- **Speed:** Request to disbursement time
- **Transparency:** Appraisal process, pricing clarity
- **Overall Experience:** Would use again?

**Minimum Sample:** 300 responses (60% response rate assumed)

### 3. Average Ticket Size

| Metric | Target | Acceptable Range | Red Flag |
|--------|--------|-----------------|----------|
| **Avg. Ticket Size** | >Rs 1,00,000 | Rs 80K-1.2L | <Rs 50,000 |

**Why this matters:**
- Doorstep economics only viable at Rs 1L+ (cost <1%)
- If avg. ticket size is Rs 50K, service is subsidized
- Low ticket size indicates wrong customer segment targeted

### 4. Same-Day Disbursement Rate

| Metric | Target | Acceptable Range | Red Flag |
|--------|--------|-----------------|----------|
| **Same-Day %** | >90% | 85-95% | <70% |

**Definition:** Loan amount credited to customer account within 24 hours of doorstep visit

**Failure Reasons to Track:**
- Technical issues (app/system downtime)
- Gold appraisal discrepancy requiring re-verification
- Customer documentation incomplete
- Transit delay (traffic/logistics)

**Mitigation:** Any <90% month triggers root cause analysis

### 5. Gold Transit Incidents

| Metric | Target | Acceptable Range | Red Flag |
|--------|--------|-----------------|----------|
| **Incidents** | 0 | 0 | ANY incident |

**Zero-tolerance policy:**
- Any gold loss/theft/damage â†’ Immediate pilot review

- Insurance claim processed within 48 hours
- Customer compensated at claimed value (no dispute)

## Monthly Review Cadence

### Week 4 (End of Month 1): First Data Review
**Focus:** Operational readiness
- Are executives trained adequately?
- Is technology working smoothly?
- Any immediate red flags?

**Action:** Fix critical bugs, refine processes

### Week 8 (End of Month 2): Go/No-Go Checkpoint
**Focus:** Early traction signals
- Are we getting leads?
- Is conversion rate reasonable?
- Is NPS positive?

**Action:**
- If RED flags â†’ Consider pause/pivot
- If YELLOW â†’ Double down on marketing
- If GREEN â†’ Proceed confidently

### Week 12 (End of Month 3): Optimization Review
**Focus:** Unit economics and customer feedback
- Is CAC trending toward target?
- What are top customer complaints?
- Which city is performing best?

**Action:**
- Reallocate resources to high-performing cities
- Fix process bottlenecks
- Adjust marketing messaging

### Week 24 (End of Month 6): Scale Decision
**Focus:** Final verdict
- Have we hit 500 loans?
- Are primary metrics ALL green?
- What's the business case for scale?

**Action:** Present to Product & Strategy Committee

## Scale Decision Framework

### Scenario 1: ALL METRICS GREEN âœ…
**Criteria Met:**
- CAC <Rs 10,000 âœ“
- NPS >4.0 âœ“
- Avg. ticket size >Rs 1L âœ“
- Same-day disbursement >90% âœ“
- Zero transit incidents âœ“

**Decision:** SCALE to 15 cities
**Investment:** Rs 15 Cr (Phase 2)
**Timeline:** 6 months for rollout
**Expected Impact:** 3,000 doorstep loans/year by end of Year 2

### Scenario 2: MIXED METRICS ğŸŸ¡
**Criteria:**
- 2-3 metrics green, others yellow
- No red flags
- Clear path to improvement

**Decision:** OPTIMIZE & EXTEND
**Action:**
- 3-month extension of pilot
- Targeted fixes for yellow metrics
- Retest before scale decision

**Investment:** Rs 1 Cr additional (operating costs)

### Scenario 3: MAJORITY RED âŒ
**Criteria:**
- CAC >Rs 15,000 (uneconomical)
- NPS <3.5 (poor customer experience)
- Avg. ticket <Rs 50K (wrong segment)
- Multiple operational failures

**Decision:** PIVOT OR EXIT
**Options:**
1. **Pivot to Partner Model:** License Rupeek/Indiagold for doorstep, Kotak provides capital
2. **Exit Doorstep:** Focus on branch + digital channels only
3. **Niche Play:** Offer doorstep only for >Rs 2L loans (premium segment)
