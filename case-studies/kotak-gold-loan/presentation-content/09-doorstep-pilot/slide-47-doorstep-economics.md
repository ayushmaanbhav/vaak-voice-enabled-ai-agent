### Break-Even Analysis
**Target:** 500 doorstep loans in 6 months
- **Monthly target:** 84 loans
- **Effective cost per transaction:** Rs 8,600 (including fixed costs)
- **At Rs 1L average ticket size:** 8.6% of loan amount
- **Revenue from 10% yield on Rs 1L for 1 year:** Rs 10,000
- **Gross margin:** Rs 1,400 per loan

## Cost Optimization Strategies

### Year 1 (Pilot)
- Higher cost per transaction due to low volume
- Focus on learning and process refinement
- Premium pricing justified by convenience

### Year 2 (Scale)
- 3x volume → Cost drops to Rs 3,000 per transaction
- Shared resources across cities
- Route optimization reduces travel costs

### Year 3 (Maturity)
- 5x volume → Cost drops to Rs 2,000 per transaction
- Amortization complete on devices
- Process automation reduces manpower needs
